What is Semantic Matching?
Our system uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) vectorization combined with Cosine Similarity to measure how closely a resume matches a job description beyond just keyword counting.
How It Works

Text Preprocessing

Resume and job description are cleaned, tokenized, and normalized. Stop words removed, text lowercased.

TF-IDF Vectorization

Both texts are converted into numerical vectors using TF-IDF, capturing word importance and uniqueness.

Cosine Similarity

The angle between vectors is calculated. Score ranges from 0 (no match) to 1 (perfect match).

Combined Scoring

Semantic score is combined with skills match, experience match, education match, and resume quality for final ranking.
