Radar Chart - Match Score Breakdown

Shows 5 dimensions: Skills Match, Experience Match, Education Match, Semantic Similarity, and Resume Quality. Each axis represents a scoring component.

Word Cloud - Resume Keywords

Visual representation of the most frequent words in a resume. Larger words appear more often, highlighting key skills and experience areas.

Quality Gauge - Resume Score

Speedometer-style gauge showing overall resume quality score (0-100). Green zone = excellent, Yellow = good, Red = needs improvement.

Ranking Bar Chart - All Candidates

Horizontal bar chart comparing all candidates by total match score. Color-coded: Green (excellent >=70%), Yellow (good 50-69%), Red (<50%).

Skills Comparison Chart

Side-by-side comparison showing matched skills (green) vs missing skills (red) for each candidate against job requirements.
